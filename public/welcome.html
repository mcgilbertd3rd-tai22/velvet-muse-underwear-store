FAQ

Everything you'd like to know.

What does Velvet Muse sell?

We exclusively curate luxury intimate apparel — bras, panties, underwear and full lingerie sets. We do not stock any other product categories.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are dispatched within 24 hours and arrive in 3–7 business days worldwide, always in unbranded, discreet packaging.

What is your return policy?

Unworn pieces with tags attached may be returned within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Sale items can be exchanged.

How do I find my size?

Every product page includes a detailed fit guide. Our customer care team is also available via WhatsApp for one-on-one fittings.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. All payments are processed through Pay4Work Merchant with bank-grade encryption. We never store your card details.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — Velvet Muse ships to over 80 countries. Customs duties may apply depending on your destination.
